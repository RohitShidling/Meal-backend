2. BUG: Cart checkout crashes with 500 (NEW BUG)
   - Error: "FOR UPDATE cannot be applied to the nullable side of an outer join"
   - Route: POST /api/client/payment/checkout-cart returning 500
   - File: src/client/controllers/paymentController.js lines 444-451
   - Root cause: The query does LEFT JOIN meal_sizes ms followed by FOR UPDATE
     on the entire result set. PostgreSQL does not allow FOR UPDATE when a 
     LEFT JOIN is present because the nullable side cannot be locked.
   - Fix: Change FOR UPDATE to FOR UPDATE OF ci, s (scope to specific tables)
     or remove the LEFT JOIN and fetch meal_size_name separately.

3. BUG: Nested CTE in getPaymentStats is invalid SQL (NOT FIXED)
   - File: src/admin/controllers/paymentController.js lines 329-332
   - The recentPayments query nests a WITH CTE inside a FROM (...) subquery.
     PostgreSQL does not allow WITH clauses inside subqueries.
   - Currently returning 200 but will break with certain data or PG versions.
   - Fix: Move the CTE to the top level of the query, not inside FROM (...).
